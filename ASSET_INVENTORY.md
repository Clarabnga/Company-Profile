# Garda Karya Nusantara Asset Inventory

Development-only handoff inventory for final client-supplied assets. The frontend currently reserves intentional blank containers; it does not generate, download, or publish substitute imagery.

## Rules

- Do not show filenames, `placeholder`, or `waiting asset` copy to visitors.
- Use authentic Indonesian corporate, security, facility, logistics, hospitality, and recruitment contexts.
- Do not show weapons, competitor marks, identifiable client branding, sensitive documents, or unlicensed artwork.
- Record ownership, licence, subject consent, and brand approval before publication.
- Keep important subjects within the central 60% safe area for responsive cropping.
- Existing files in `public/images/photos/` and legacy SVG illustrations are not approved final assets.

## Replacement QA

1. Match the exact filename and section above.
2. Confirm source, rights, consent, and brand approval.
3. Inspect focal crops at 1440, 1280, 1024, 768, 390, and 375 pixel viewports.
4. Confirm no weapon, competitor mark, personal data, or misleading branch claim is visible.
5. Confirm colour treatment stays within navy, natural green, cream, neutral grey, and controlled orange.
6. Run responsive and accessibility QA before marking an asset final.
